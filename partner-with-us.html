At Toon Tales we believe in the magic that partnerships bring about. We collaborate with brands, agencies and production houses to bring animation to ideas with our signature touch

Why Partners with Us?

Team Tales

We are a full-service independent animation studio, going strong since 2019. Our team is power packed with Illustrators, Animators, Directors, Designers, Editors, Producers, Compositors, Visual Effects Artists, Motion Capture specialists, and Sound Engineers, each hand-picked and trained in our unique workflow.

Speed Ninjas

In this fast moving marketing landscape our speedy turnaround and spot-on execution combined with a vast library of Characters, Backgrounds and Props custom created over the last two years, add that pop to your visual story.

Customer Centric

From Advertisers to Consumer Brands, we create standout creative solutions for everyone. By employing local teams from across the country, we offer a cultural match, which enhances brand awareness so you can get the best possible results for your creative for social media, digital broadcast or brand storytelling.

Production Houses
