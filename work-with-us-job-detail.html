2D Animator

Explore Creative Collaboration

We are looking for a creative, hands on 2D Animator to join One Media's Animation division to bring fresh,
original millennial content to life.

Type of Position

Full Time

Division/Department

2D Animation Team

Job Responsibilities

Visualize scripts/lyrics to create conceptual sketches and visual storyboards suited for 2D Animation
End to end Character Animation, working with character rigs and refining their range of movements, expressions to bring the characters to life
Interpret and work with voice overs and audio, understand emotional cues and find rhythm in the narrative to create appropriate visuals
Composite the various layers of animation (backgrounds, characters and graphics) in order to produce the finished piece and ensure consistency in style and quality
Editing and adding subtitles
Must work cohesively with the creative team and complete content within timelines

Job Requirements

At least 1 Year experience as a 2D Animator in a studio environment
Must have a strong understanding of the 12 Animation Principles
Experience with Storyboarding, Character Animation and Compositing is a must
Must have hands on experience with animation tools within the Adobe Suite especially with Adobe After Effects character animation or must be willing to transition to this tool.
Experience with hand drawn animation is a bonus
Great eye for detail to ensure the timing, expressions and movements of characters required to do justice to every story
Video editing skills
Must be a team player and work within deadlines
